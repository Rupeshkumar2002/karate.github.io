The most well-known types of Karate around the world. Shotokan Karate was developed in Tokyo by the Father of Modern Karate- Gichin Funakoshi. This is one of the main Karate styles that includes very strong and linear techniques. This Karate style has 70% hard techniques and 30% soft movements. The Shotokan Karate kata is 26 in number and these must all be learnt by the students. In this form of Karate, the beginner stances tend to be deep and these become longer as the student advances. The focus here is on speed, form, balance and breathing while the student performs the various techniques.
